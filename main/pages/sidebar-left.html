Elevate Technologies

Product Manager (March 2023 - Present)

Elevate Technologies is a business analytics company that introduces machine learning from their software to combat elevator breakdowns and expensive maintenance requests.

At Elevate, I worked on various new product MVPs and built the roadmap for the mobile app while we made the transition into developing the Saas Platform. During this time, I used various project management softwares to encourage organized collaboration amongst the designers and engineers.

Elevate App

Using XCode, I designed and connected the front-end to the backend for our MVP. The MVP's functionality included 4 screens, enabled location services, and real-time updates to the AWS Server. Our physical model that connected to the Elevate App's call function was made from servos and an arduino built onto the elevator buttons.

This MVP got support from Indiana University's Facility Services Manager, Kirk Lowry, and continued praise from Homeland Security. The first two beta tests were in two Indiana University elevators with over 50 users and 200 successful elevator calls.

Tools Used:

XCode(Swift), Figma, Procreate, ClickUp, GitHub

Elevate Software (Saas)

I created the MVP for the Elevate Software (Saas) to test engagement with building managers and Homeland Security team. The prototype is fully functional and simulates getting updates from maintenance requests and AI-Generated Alerts based off of inconsistencies in data collection.

Tools Used:

Adobe Illustrator, Figma, Procreate, ClickUp, GitHub

At NOYACK, I worked directly with CEO, CJ Folini, to determine the first two stages for the Mobility Hub project. It was a crucial point in the project where I was given 3 commercial real-estate properties to develop partnerships and implement EV Charging and Cold Storage components.

My first endeavor was the Leveque garage. I gathered over 5000 prospects for the EV Charging and Cold Storage partnerships and integration models. I determined maintenance estimations for investors and copabilities of the parking garage. I curated a 10 page spreadsheet with relevant nearby artifacts and important usages for the Leveque Mobility Hub.

While working with CJ, I also worked heavily on the plan fro NOYACK Logistics to become part of the Next-Generation of Wealth Managers. I provided detailed market research in the 2022 Q3-Q4 update for the "Next-Steps" plan.
