Keyshort.AI

Founder, CEO (June 2023 - Present)

Keyshort.AI is an information technology startup that helps users with their productivity.

Keyshort.AI uses AI and Machine Learning technology in order to collect keyboard shortcuts for specific websites across the web. With this information, we incorporate it into our databases that the Keyshort Chatbot can retrieve when the user asks any for keyboard shortcuts. The machine learning technology is constantly improving Keyshort's chatbot accuracy to provide the exact shortcut for the user 100% of the time.

Keyshort.AI is currently in the pre-seed investment stage where we are focused on creating a solid working product for our users. As of July 31st, 2023, we were able to launch our unlisted BETA Version to our interest list of 20 users. During this initial launch, I will be collecting lots of user feedback and any bug issues that arise during their time using this 1.0 version.

The Beginning of Keyshort

Keyshort.AI's idea was originally introduced by my older sister, Elizabeth, repeatedly asking what keyboard shortcut would do a specific function on some of her animation applications. With no knowledge of these applications whatsoever, I would go on a deep search on the web to find a simple command usually hidden in a big data table. This sparked my idea to create a product that would simplify that process and provide an all-in-one capsule for keyboard shortcuts on a plethora of different websites on the internet.

The Gleeful Globe

Founder, Product Manager (August 2020 - January 2022)

The Gleeful Globe was a positive news channel that was started by Derek in the midst of COVID-19 in order to combat and outweigh the overbearing negative news at the time.

The Gleeful Globe enabled peers from around the world in over 50 countries to watch positive news on the channel. We were posting videos acorss multiple platforms, most notably, TikTok. Our channel garnered over 150,000 followers within the first week of starting the channel, surpassing world-renown news channel, NBC News.

Derek's part on The Gleeful Globe was to produce videos daily along with understanding the algorithm for TikTok for 2020-2021. During this time, he documented "hot zones" for when to post, and also the understanding of how to maximize hashtags and backlinking to other Gleeful Globe videos.

The Gleeful Globe's Team

Derek gathered some of his friends during COVID-19 to have an entirely virtual organization that created content for The Gleeful Globe. It was important that Derek's vision for the product growth was strong and Carly Luu and Dan Than were onboard. Carly was an amazing producer that created the cutest content by choosing curated content that Dan choose from news channels all across the world. Derek worked with both Carly and Dan to teach them to understand how to utilize the TikTok algorithm.



The Beginning of The Gleeful Globe

The Gleeful Globe began when I was walking around the house in the COVID-19 pandemic and watched about 20 seconds of news. One after another, I saw devastatingly sad news during the time. I wanted to have a change, there can still be positive news during hard times and bringing some positivity is not going to take away from the importance of other current events. To start this, I posted the first Gleeful Globe video on August 31, 2023. From there, the videos gained thousands of viewers and likes bringing the channel to where it is now.

Derek specifically studied the TikTok algorithm in 2020 creating spreadsheets of best times to post based on location, demographic, and news topic. The success grew by following his research and he was able to teach his team how to utilize the algorithm.

Stone House LLC

Business Consultant & Designer (August 2020 - January 2022)

Stone House LLC is a commercial real estate company in Puerto Rico with 13 units where I was given tasks for various properties to make crucial decisions for design and property choice.



Finca Brisa Del Rio Dome

For the Finca Brisa Del Rio Dome, I was tasked to develop the brand for the property as it was newly developed in Humacao, Puerto Rico.

The problem with the dome prior to launch was that the photos were not properly showcasing the different experiences on the 16-acre farm.

I wanted to showcase the different areas on the farm ranging from Koi Fish Farming, Exotic Fruit Trees, and the Petting Zoo. I took photos using drones, GoPros, and the Canon EOS Rebel T7. Along with this, I brought a team to create a website for separate bookings outside of AirBnb to begin growing the brand for Stone House LLC.

The outcome of this project is successfully recruiting a new member onto the Stone House LLC team to continue developing the brand on Facebook, AirBnb, and Youtube.

Timeline: 6 month (January 2021-June 2021)



Guava House

For the Guava House, I was tasked to create the entire layout for furniture and electrical for 2 of the apartment units.

I used the SmartDraw software to layout the design. I needed to find out a way to have an open-concept apartment with the difficulty of 2'x 2' pillars placed at every third of the room.

I decided to keep the bathroom close to the door, nearer to the electrical and plumbing source, also out of view when the guests open the door. Then for the kitchen and beds I wanted to use the pillars as the divider emulating a spacious room.

Timeline: Ongoing (June 2023-Present)
